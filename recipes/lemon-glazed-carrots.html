Recipe for Lemon Glazed Carrots

Description :

A hassle-free and healthy snack option to satisfy your craving

Both the ingredients and procedure are very simple and affordable

Hope you enjoy! Lets us know your feedback...

Ingredients :

  • 2 carrots, chopped into 4 strips each
  • 1 tablespoon -
    • Butter
    • Brown sugar
    • lemon juice
  • Add salt & pepper to your preference

Steps

  1. Place carrots into a large pot and cover with water; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until carrots are tender, about 8 minutes.
  3. Then Drain.
  4. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Cook and stir carrots, brown sugar, and lemon juice in the melted butter.
  6. Stir often, until sugar has dissolved.
